What HMS is. And what it isn't.

The first twelve months require a knife, not a buffet. Lock a vertical, lock a geography, lock a promise — then expand on proof, not hope.

HMS is
  • YesA Florida-first lead partner for final-expense life-insurance agents.
  • YesA full-stack delivery team — creative, media buying, landing pages, CRM, VA follow-up — under one roof.
  • YesA proprietary intent-data layer via ArkData / ListMagic that no other FE vendor runs.

HMS is not
  • NoA home services agency. Not roofers, landscapers, HVAC. That fight is lost on price.
