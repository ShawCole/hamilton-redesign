Questions people ask

The honest answers.

Do I need a medical exam?
No. Every carrier we represent for final expense offers no-exam coverage. Guaranteed-issue policies ask zero health questions. Simplified-issue policies ask a short set — if you answer "no" to all, you're approved.
What does it actually cost?
For most Florida seniors ages 60–75, a $10,000–$15,000 policy runs $24–$58/month. Exact rate depends on age, tobacco, coverage amount, and carrier. The form above gives you a real quote in 60 seconds.
What if I get declined?
If a simplified-issue carrier declines you, we switch to guaranteed-issue — which cannot decline anyone in the eligible age band. You will get a policy.
How fast does my family receive the money?
Typically within 5–10 business days of the carrier receiving the death certificate. Paid directly — never through probate. Some carriers offer expedited payment for funeral home direct-assignment.
Can the rate go up later?
No. Your monthly premium is locked at the rate you sign up at, for the life of the policy. It cannot be raised based on age or health changes.
What carriers do you work with?
Mutual of Omaha, Gerber Life, Aetna, Transamerica, Americo, AIG, Prosperity Life, and more. All A+ rated. Jane shops them for you — we don't force one carrier.
